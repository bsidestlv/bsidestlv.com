## Rules and Guidelines

1. Only physically present individuals or teams may **claim a prize**
   - The winning team gets a collective prize shared between participants
   - Make sure a member of your team is in the announcement ceremony!
   - Only in-person members will be eligible for a personal trophy
   - Each team may include up to 5 participants (remote CTF); more participants allowed but only 5 awarded
   - Winners will be contacted via the email registered at the CTF Website — use a real/active email!
   - This is a game, play fairly — we reserve the right to disqualify participants
2. The prize for local challenges is personal

> We usually keep challenges alive for a couple more days after the event to enable write-ups.

## FAQ

**Do I have to be a member of a team to participate?**

**Is the CTF aimed at beginners?**
Some challenges will be at beginner level, some at expert level. This year we also have a special treasure hunt for "zero knowledge" participants.

## Challenge Types

This year we have 3 kinds of challenges:

1. **Remote challenges** — classic online CTF
2. **Local challenges** — on site
3. **"Zero knowledge" treasure hunt** — on site, for juniors

---

## Rules and Guidelines

1. Only physically present teams may claim a prize — make sure a member of your team is in the announcement ceremony!
2. We will contact winners using the email registered at the CTF Website — use a real/active email
3. This is a game, play fairly — we reserve the right to disqualify participants
4. Each team may be made of one or more individuals
5. The winning team will get a collective prize shared between team participants
6. We usually keep challenges alive for a couple more days after the event to enable write-ups

## FAQ

**Do I have to be a member of a team to participate?**
No, you can register as a team of one member.

**Is the CTF aimed at beginners?**
Some challenges will be at beginner level, some at expert level. This year we also have a special treasure hunt for "zero knowledge" participants.

**Are there binary exploitation challenges?**
Yes. Yes there are.

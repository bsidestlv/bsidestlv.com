## Rules and Guidelines

1. Remote participation is allowed (for all teams)
2. Only physically present teams may claim a prize — make sure a member of your team is in the announcement ceremony!
3. We will contact winners using the email registered at the CTF Website — use an email you monitor
4. This is a game, play fairly — we reserve the right to disqualify participants
5. Each team may be made of one or more individuals
6. Most web challenges use a sticky cookie called `BSidesTLV` — make sure to work with sessions
7. Hourly cleanup of large and old files for persistency challenges
8. Recommended team size is 5 (limited to 10)
9. We usually keep challenges alive for ~1 week after the event to enable write-ups

## FAQ

**Do I have to be a member of a team to participate?**
No, you can register as a team of one member.

**Is the CTF aimed at beginners?**
Some challenges will be at beginner level, some at expert level. Try solving from the lower scores working up.

**Are there binary exploitation challenges?**
Yes. Yes there are.

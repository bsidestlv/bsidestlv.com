## AI Hacking Village

The AI Hacking Village at BSidesTLV explores the intersection of artificial intelligence and cybersecurity, focusing on AI security, adversarial machine learning, and AI-powered security tools.

### What to Expect

- **Adversarial ML Workshops** — Learn how to attack and defend AI systems
- **AI Security Research** — Explore vulnerabilities in machine learning models
- **AI-Powered Tools** — Discover how AI is transforming security research
- **Prompt Engineering** — Master techniques for working with LLMs securely
- **Ethical AI Hacking** — Understand responsible disclosure in AI security

### Activities

- Hands-on adversarial attack demonstrations
- AI model security assessments
- Workshops on prompt injection and jailbreaking
- Discussions on the future of AI in cybersecurity
- Networking with AI security researchers

### Who Should Attend

Ideal for security researchers interested in AI/ML security, AI practitioners wanting to secure their models, bug hunters exploring AI vulnerabilities, and anyone curious about the security implications of AI.
